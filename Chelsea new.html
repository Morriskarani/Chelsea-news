Pochettino's Statement Ahead of Newcastle Match

Chelsea coach, Pochettino, has declared that the team will go into their next match against Newcastle United with full force and energy. Despite the coach saying, "The performances have been very good, I’m so happy with the performances," he acknowledges the fans' frustration, stating, "If the fans want to listen, perfect. If not, what can I do? They’ll keep booing. But I’m giving my best to this team. Boo me, not the players. It's fine."

Frustrations Among Chelsea Players

Internal frustrations are building among Chelsea players due to inconsistent results. Despite being in a mid-table position, the team is eagerly anticipating success in the FA Cup and aiming for a higher position in the league table.
